Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety is a natural response to stress, characterized by feelings of worry, nervousness, or fear. It can be triggered by various situations, such as exams, job interviews, or social interactions. While occasional anxiety is normal, excessive and persistent anxiety can interfere with daily life.

Managing anxiety involves self-care, relaxation techniques, and seeking support when needed. If anxiety becomes overwhelming, professional help can provide effective coping strategies.
